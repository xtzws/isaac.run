If I Told Him: A Completed Portrait of Picasso

“If I Told Him” is a literary portrait by Gertrude Stein (1874-1946) of Pablo Picasso (1881-1973), in response to a portrait he painted of her about twenty years prior. Here is what she had to say about her portrait: I was and I still am satisfied with my portrait, for me, it is I, and it is the only reproduction of me which is always I, for me.

— “Portrait of Gertrude Stein” by Pablo Picasso. Oil on canvas, 1905. Read more at The Met.
